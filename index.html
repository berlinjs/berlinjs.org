Berlin.JS is a usergroup focused on JavaScript and related topics. We meet regularly on the 3rd Thursday each month at 7p.m. at co.up Offices, Adalbertstraße 7-8 in Berlin-Kreuzberg.

Next meetup on August 21st

Cocos2d-js: Game development for Android, Ios, Web

Michael Schmidt

One code base for all platforms, (almost) native speed

Cocos2d-js is the js-Version of Cocos2d-X, which is the base of many app store top games like Hill Climb Racing or 2048. I'm developing a tetris/scrabble game and will talk about my experiences with the platform. I'll also show some code.

Code of Conduct

Long story short: Be awesome to each other.

Our goal is to have an awesome and inclusive community meetup where people meet, hang out together, chat, listen to talks, exchange ideas and make new friends. Any harmful or discriminating behaviour will not be tolerated and results in the offending person being expelled from the meetup.
